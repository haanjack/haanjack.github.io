# Syntax Guide
## Headers

# H1
## H2
### H3
#### H4

## Emphasis
*This text will be italic*
_This will also be italic_

**This text will be bold**
__This will also be bold__

_You **can** combine them_

## List
### Unordered List

* Item 1
* Item 2
  * Item 2a
  * Item 2b

### Ordered List
1. Item 1
1. Item 2
1. Item 3
   1. Item 3a
   1. Item 3b

## Image

<!-- Format: ![Alt Text](url) -->
